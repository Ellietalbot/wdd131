Leaf & Thrive Site Plan

Ellie Talbot

WDD 131-3

Overview

Purpose

My site is for plant owners to help them keep track of their plants and help care for them.

Audience

Forgetful plant parents

Dynamic elements

- A welcome message A photo carasol with plant care tips A Plant Tracker: Allow users to add, update, and remove their plants from a list. Each plant could have attributes like name, species, watering schedule, and sunlight needs. Add Plant: A form where users input plant details. On submission, a new card appears. Watering Reminders: Compare the last watered date with the current date and display alerts for overdue plants. Editable Plant Cards: Allow users to edit plant details directly in the UI.
